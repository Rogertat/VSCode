A bit about me

I design and deploy machine learning systems end to end — framing the problem, building the models, and standing up the infrastructure that keeps them healthy in production.

My work spans NLP, recommendation, and computer vision — and lately, the frontier I can't put down: AI agents. LLMs that don't just answer questions but plan, call tools, write code, and check their own work — orchestrated with LangGraph, AutoGen, and MCP, grounded with retrieval, and held honest by ruthless evaluation. I love the spot where solid research meets careful engineering: reproducible experiments, clean pipelines, and monitoring that catches drift before users do.

Off the clock, I write about applied ML and mentor engineers making the jump from notebooks to production.
