About

I design and deploy machine learning systems end to end — framing the problem, building the models, and standing up the infrastructure that keeps them healthy in production.

My work spans natural language processing, recommendation, and computer vision, with a recent focus on large language models, retrieval-augmented generation, and evaluation. I like the space where solid research meets careful engineering: reproducible experiments, clean data pipelines, and monitoring that catches drift before users do.

Outside of shipping models, I enjoy writing about applied ML and mentoring engineers who are moving from notebooks into production.
