A bit about me

I design and deploy machine learning systems end to end — framing the problem, building the models, and standing up the infrastructure that keeps them healthy in production.

I love the spot where solid research meets careful engineering: reproducible experiments, clean data pipelines, and monitoring that catches drift before users do.

Off the clock, I write about applied ML and mentor engineers making the jump from notebooks to production.
